La piccola biblioteca Italiana

Welcome to La piccola biblioteca Italiana, organized by the Italian school of San Diego LLC

All books in the library are in Italian

It is currently opened to students of the school or people that donated any book to the library

How to use the library

  • Register an account specifying the number of students you have at Italian school
  • Reserve one or more books (each family can borrow a book for each student + 1 extra)
  • Pickup the books on Thursday after class
  • If you desired book is "on loan", checkout at the Biblioteca table if it has been returned
  • Return them after 2 weeks on Thursday after class, if no-one picks it up, you can renew up to 4 weeks total
  • If a book is lost or damaged, you are responbile for buying a replacement
