La piccola biblioteca Italiana

Welcome to La piccola biblioteca Italiana, a library of books in Italian located in San Diego, organized by Italian school of San Diego LLC

It is currently open to students of the school or people that donated any Italian book to the library

How to use the library

  • Register an account specifying the number of students you have at Italian school
  • Reserve one or more books (each family can borrow a book for each student + 1 extra, initially just 1 per family)
  • Pickup the books at Maura's home in University City (the system will email you the address), hours:
    • Thursday 3pm-9pm
    • Saturday 9am-9pm
    • Sunday 9am-9pm
  • If a book is not picked up by Sunday night, the reservation is automagically canceled
  • Books are due back in 2 weeks after Thursday, so you have less days if you pickup during the weekend
  • During holidays, loans are automatically extended until Italian school starts again
  • Return them before class at the Biblioteca table, if there is no request for it, you can renew up to 4 weeks total
  • If a book is lost or damaged, you are responsible for buying a replacement
