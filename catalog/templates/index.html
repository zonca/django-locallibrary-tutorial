La piccola biblioteca Italiana

Welcome to La piccola biblioteca Italiana, a library of books in Italian located in San Diego, organized by the Italian school of San Diego LLC

It is currently open to students of the school or people that donated any Italian book to the library

How to use the library

  • Register an account specifying the number of students you have at Italian school
  • Reserve one or more books (each family can borrow a book for each student + 1 extra, initially just 1 per family)
  • Pickup the books on Thursday after class
  • If your desired book is "on loan", ask at the Biblioteca table if it has been returned, if so, you can borrow it
  • Return them after 2 weeks on Thursday after class, if no-one picks it up, you can renew up to 4 weeks total
  • If a book is lost or damaged, you are responbile for buying a replacement
